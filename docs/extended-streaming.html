Request Lifetime Streaming History

Unfortunately, the Spotify API no longer supports retrieving a user's all-time streaming history directly.

However, if you'd like an analysis of your listening history over the lifespan of your account, you can request your Extended Streaming History directly from Spotify by following these steps:

  1. Click the “Request from Spotify” button below.
  2. Scroll down to the section labeled:
    “Download your Data”.
  3. Select “Extended streaming history”.
  4. Click the “Request Data” button.

Once you have the file:

  • Drag and drop the "my_spotify_data.zip" file onto this page.
  • Our tool will automatically analyze it and provide insights into your listening history!
